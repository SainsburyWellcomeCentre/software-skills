Room TBC, UCL.

Overview

This half day course will introduce the use of Python packages to analyse (numpy, scipy & pandas) and visualise (matplotlib, seaborn) neuronal timeseries data.

The aim will be to work through analysis of a single timeseries (a current recording from a neuron, where drug was applied in the second half of the recording).
